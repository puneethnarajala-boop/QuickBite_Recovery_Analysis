# QuickBite_Recovery_Analysis
🍴 QuickBite Express – Crisis Recovery Dashboard Codebasics Resume Challenge

This project is part of the Codebasics Resume Challenge. It analyses QuickBite Express, a Bengaluru-based food delivery startup, that faced a major crisis in June 2025. The goal is to provide data-driven insights to help the company recover, regain customer trust, and improve performance.

🧠 Project Overview

In June 2025, QuickBite faced a viral food safety issue and delivery outages that caused:

Drop in customer engagement

Decrease in daily orders

Surge in cancellations and delayed deliveries

Restaurants leaving the platform

The dashboard helps management understand what went wrong and take steps to rebuild trust.

💡 Key Insights Covered

1️⃣ Customer Segments – Identify which customers can be recovered and which need new engagement strategies. 2️⃣ Order Trends – Compare pre-crisis, crisis, and post-crisis phases to understand behavioral changes. 3️⃣ Delivery Performance – Analyse delivery times, SLA compliance, and cancellations to find gaps. 4️⃣ Campaign Opportunities – Recommend cities and user groups for targeted marketing. 5️⃣ Restaurant Partnerships – Discover which restaurants are most valuable for long-term retention. 6️⃣ Feedback & Sentiment – Track customer satisfaction and online reviews to measure trust recovery.
